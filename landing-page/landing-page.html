What is Maestro?

Maestro is a new management platform for scheduling and publishing multimedia contents across web, mobile and on-location.

Background Music

Customize your audio experience from our expansive media library. Choose pre-determined playlists or create your very own.

Radio

Brand your business with radio, publish across web, mobile and on-location. High-level production & support at your fingertips.

Digital Signage

Combine BGM & Radio with Digital Signage. A perfect way to leverage customer attrition and product placement while promoting.

Cloud Platform

Maestro Cloud enables you to schedule content with ease helping you effectively manage hardware devices on-location.
